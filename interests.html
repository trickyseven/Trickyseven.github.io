Interests

The first thing to know about me is that there are several things to know about me. I have a broad range of professional and personal skills and interests. I've provided a list of interests below.

Professional

Data Analysis

One of my greatest strengths is problem solving using data. In both professional and academic settings, I have used data to provide strategic recommendations to help aid in decision making. This typically involves regression analysis, crosstabulation of data, and other statistical performance measures. Some examples of my work are presented below.

  • I led a team that consulted a company looking to enter the US professional chef market. My team conducted interviews, created surveys, analyzed the data, and finally wrote a report detailing our recommendation.
  • I have performed detailed backward looking analyses that have been used to monitor sales and marketing efforts for professional organizations. These typically are then used as predictive analytical tools to determine where to focus efforts next.
  • I have deep experience with CRM systems and mining data within those systems for product development purposes.

Marketing

I have a solid background in marketing and branding. I have consulted for a company looking to become more consumer focused. In the future, I anticipate learning about digital marketing.

Technical Writing

I have four years of experience writing proposals for potential investors in real estate. I have also written papers on using game theory to understand war time actions.

Process Improvement

I have focused my coursework on ways to improve processes as well as resources to better achieve objectives.

Personal

Reading

I love to read and enjoy learning new topics through both fiction and nonfiction. Some of my favorite authors are Chuck Palahniuk, Cormac McCarthy, Malcolm Gladwell, Elmore Leonard, Neil Gaiman, and Terry Pratchett.

Some of my favorite past times are outdoor pursuits, such as running, camping, hiking, and golf. I enjoy the outdoors and love spending time in the wilderness.
